do

Like to create? So do we.

Since our inception, we have strived to provide a space that allows students to succeed with their peers as a team. Robotics isn't exactly easy, but that's what makes us stronger. From our seasoned design leads to our young and enterprising engineers, we welcome anyone willing to put themselves to the test and compete versus thousands of bright minds from around the world. In the coming years, we will grow, take on new competitions, and complete more projects.

We are a community of designers, creators, and innovators. We are RoboBears.

What is RoboGames?

RoboGames is the zenith of international competitive robotics, held annually in California. Their more than 50 events range from autonomous soccer-playing robots to heavy steel battlebots. It is here that RoboBears will be competing both this year and in the future.

Our Event

This year, we'll be competing in the 60 lb combat event. This consists of designing and building a radio-controlled robot that will engage in combat with other robots until destruction or the end of the round.

Pictures on this page taken from RoboGames.

What's Next?

As we grow, RoboBears will compete in more events at RoboGames and beyond. Our team is extremely diverse, and we fully expect our talents to propel us farther into fields such as machine learning, signal processing, and feedback control. Follow our team as we delve into these topics!
